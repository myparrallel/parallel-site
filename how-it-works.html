The core promise

You don't need
the right words.
Just the honest ones.

Type how you actually feel — not clinical labels. "I feel empty and nothing helps" finds the same people as "major depressive disorder." Your language is valid.

The Parallel Filter shows you exactly who has been through your experience. The thread graphic represents two paths converging — yours and theirs.

  • Fuzzy search — typos and variations all work
  • First-person language — "I feel depressed" works just like "depressed"
  • Clinical tags are backend only — users only ever see human language
  • Multiple experiences can be filtered simultaneously

Sacred space

Say the thing.
Watch it disappear.

Venting is the one feature on Parallel with no Sage scanner, no feedback, no moderation. It's just you and the release. When the timer runs out — it's gone.

Three modes, three moods. Free users get Float. Paid users choose how their words leave.

  • Float — words drift upward and dissolve as you type them
  • Heartbeat — screen pulses faster as time runs out
  • Burn — write everything, watch the timer, it all dissolves at once
  • Crisis detection still runs silently — you're never truly alone
  • Nothing is stored. Nothing is shared. Between you and you.

AI done right

Holds space.
Never center stage.

Sage is Parallel's built-in AI — always labeled, always transparent. It's there in the gap between posting and your community arriving. It checks in when posts carry weight. It never pretends to be human.

The SmartTextBox scans your response tone silently as you type. After a 2.5 second pause, Sage reads what you wrote. If it flags something — a gentle nudge appears. You always choose whether to post.

  • Style is never flagged — slang, AAVE, casual language all fine
  • Only flags what would make someone feel dismissed or unseen
  • Crisis detection routes to real resources — never a chatbot loop
  • No tone scanning inside Venting — ever
  • Always labeled "Parallel AI" — no ambiguity

Every DM thread shows your shared parallel — the experience that connected you — so you never lose sight of why you're here.

  • Available at Rooted tier and above

Communication skills

Learn to show up
for anyone.

The Support Center teaches you how to actually be helpful — not just on Parallel, but in your life. Articles, quick reference guides, and a live Sage tone scanner that grades your responses before you post.

These skills transfer. The way you learn to listen here will change how you show up for your family, your friends, your relationships.

  • Articles on grief, anger, crisis, and giving feedback without fixing
  • Quick reference cards — phrases that work, phrases to avoid
